Our Story

A cafe built for
community and comfort

Suto Cafe was born from a simple belief — that everyone deserves a beautiful, welcoming space to slow down, connect, and enjoy something truly good.

Located in the heart of Aurangpura, Sambhajinagar, we have become the go-to spot for students, professionals, couples, and everyone in between. We're not just a cafe. We're your neighbourhood second home.

Every item on our menu is prepared with care. Every corner of our space is designed with intention. And every customer who walks in is family.
